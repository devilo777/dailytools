Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Zero Data Collection Principle

At DailyTools Hub, privacy is our top priority. All tools provided on this platform (including PDF converters, image compressors, and password generators) process data locally within your web browser. We do not upload, store, or view your files, data, or personal text on any server.

2. Local Storage & Theme Configuration

We use your browser's local storage solely to remember user configuration preferences, such as your Light or Dark Mode choice. This data never leaves your device.

3. Third-Party Services & Cookies

We may use basic analytics services or display non-intrusive ads (like Google AdSense) to keep this platform free forever. These service providers may use cookies to customize your experience.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or feedback regarding this policy, feel free to contact us via our contact page.
